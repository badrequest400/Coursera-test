Architecture for all

2018.09.28


For the third time Open House Zurich is opening doors to visitors to take free architectural tours of our city. The event will take place on September 29 and 30.

We would like to invite you to visit the 2000 Watt settlement Kalkbreite, to marvel at the Ballet Mécanique or to find peace and quiet in the New Aposotolic Church Albisrieden. Why not combine a Sunday walk along the Limmat with a detour to the Speich area? Or enjoy the panoramic view of northern Zurich from the Leutschenbach school building? The Villa Patumbah once again shows its magnificent interior. Let yourself be surprised!

Source: luechingermeyer.ch
